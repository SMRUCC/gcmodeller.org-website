GCModeller is an open source systems biology software toolkit that implemented by Microsoft's .NET Framework technology and written in Microsoft VisualBasic.NET language.
We are trying to found out how does the disease was caused by the plant pathogen Xanthomonas campestris pathovar campestris on their host.
The efforts of what we does on the GCModeller developed includes: important disease pathway calculation, predicts for the important regulator of the virulence, stress resistance calculation of the bacteria.
The future work on GCModeller development will focus on bacteria/environment interaction, metagenomics and Microsoft Azure cloud computing, trying to explain the evolution of the diseases caused bacteria genome.
